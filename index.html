Christ's may ball 2020   /  Tuesday 16th June

Urgent Announcement

Please see the below message from the Christ's May Ball 2020 Committee

Dear Ticketholder,

We regret to inform you that due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, we have taken the difficult decision to cancel Christ’s May Ball 2020. We have come to this decision after careful consideration and dialogue with Christ’s College, the May Ball Presidents’ Committee, and the University of Cambridge.

We would like to express our personal thanks to all of those involved in planning the Ball thus far, particularly to the Christ’s May Ball Committee and Dr Helena Browne, our Senior Treasurer. We are incredibly proud of what we had put together up until this stage, but out of respect to our contractors, staff, and most importantly you, our guests, we feel that the event’s cancellation is the correct course of action to take.

In the coming weeks, we will be back in touch with a comprehensive refund policy. Until this point, we would be grateful for your patience.

Best wishes,
Patrick Moran and Matt Lee
Presidents, Christ’s May Ball 2020
