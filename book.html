Christ's College May Ball

bout

The committee of Christ’s College May Ball invites you to partake in a night of escapism, as you immerse yourself in a fantastical world of dragons, mazes and fairytale castles. Think crowns and armoured knights juxtaposed with sorcery and Sleeping Beauty. On Tuesday 19th June, join us on our quest which threads through the grounds of Christ’s College. Explore the Medieval Castle, enjoy the plentiful culinary delights - but beware the fiery dragon’s breath. Our committee hopes that you will enjoy the evening, and come away with your very own entertaining stories of your time at Christ’s College’s ‘A Night’s Tale.’

ood & Drink

This year, Christ’s May Ball aims to build upon our existing reputation by bringing you a collection of food and drink fit for a king. You will want for nothing, as you are served a munificent four-course banquet, followed by a spell-binding night of delights. There is a feast on offer for all who choose to embark on this medieval quest, with our bountiful selection of food indulging your every royal desire. Whether you want a swig of ale to go alongside your hog roast or a tantalizingly-tasty concoction made by the highest quality potion-masters, we guarantee to keep you sufficiently satiated throughout your evening. Christ’s May Ball consistently exceeds expectations with its magnificent culinary experiences and so will ensure your fairytale ending.

Christ's College Mayball is supporting the following charity this year...

Jimmy’s Cambridge

Jimmy’s Cambridge operates a number of projects across the city of Cambridge, for men and women who would otherwise be homeless. Their mission is not just about offering a roof, but to provide a safe environment where guests, staff, volunteers and partners can work together to create a focus on opportunity, choice and change.Jimmy's have worked with around 7,000 individuals and help them to re-build confidence and a new future. http://www.jimmyscambridge.org.uk

How are we fundraising?

You will have the option of donating to Jimmy's at ticket checkout! We will also donate name change fees too!

Want to help make Christ's May Ball a safe and memorable night for everyone?
Want to earn some money during may week?
Check out our worker roles, both full-time and half-time positions, for a chance to get involved with our may ball...

Full-time Paid Roles:

Area Manager (avg. £80 for the night)

This role will require you to supervise the other workers within the college for the duration of the ball, ensuring that your given area remains safe, clean and operating smoothly. Ideally, those with previous experience in managing others would be desired.

Hit Squad Manager - approx. £88 (£72) for the night

Members of the squad will be appointed to areas where additional staffing is required, or in emergent situations. Staff will need to adapt & respond quickly to new situations. As Hit Squad manager, you will be required to organise and manage the team. Previous experience in similar roles would be ideal.

Food & Drink Worker - approx. £82 (£66) for the night

Our food and drink personnel will be required to prepare or serve the food and drinks provided at different stalls. Additionally, they will be tasked with restocking the stalls and contributing to the friendly atmosphere of the ball.

Stage Manager - approx. £85 (£68) for the night

Our stage managers will be in charge of the smooth running of the entertainment stages throughout the ball. Strong organisational and communication skills will be required, as our stage managers will need to communicate with our Sound Monitor, Ents and Logistics teams.

Ents Runner - approx. £83 (£66) for the night.

Our runners will be needed to assist the arrival of our performers on the night, particularly when escorting them to green rooms/stages in good time. This is key in avoiding clashes or delays in performances, so any related experience in this field would be beneficial.

Ticketing Assistant - approx. £15 (£12) from 20.30-22.30pm.

This role is required at the beginning of the ball to collect and check the tickets of our guests. You will be required to work quickly and be vigilant - this is a particularly excitable point of the ball! You will be contributing to the overall atmosphere of the ball, therefore friendliness and polite interactions are greatly encouraged.

Recovery Team Supervisor - approx. £50 (£41)

The recovery team will assist the committee in returning our college to its usual state. As a supervisor, you will be required to ensure that those in your allocated area are carrying out their tasks efficiently, whilst alerting the committee to any issues.

Recovery Team Member - approx. £48 (£39)

The recovery team will assist the committee in returning our college to its usual state. You will be require to remove and clean the stages and stalls used for the ball, in addition to a range of other tasks.

Concierge (2nd half only) - approx. £48 (£38)

This role is required to help the other workers and staff to enter the ball from our concierge desk. You will need to ensure that all personnel entering the site have the required permissions. This will include communicating with our porters, Staffing and Security department.

Sound Monitor - approx. £85 (£68) for the night.

As sound monitor, you will be required to move to different stages throughout the night and must notify the committee when sound levels exceed the required levels.

Half-On, Half-Off Roles:

Food & Drink Worker

Our food and drink personnel will be required to prepare or serve the food and drinks provided at different stalls. Additionally, they will be tasked with restocking the stalls and contributing to the friendly atmosphere of the ball.

Set Up Team Member

Before the ball, our committee will need help setting up our stalls, stages and decorating the college in preparation for the event. The roles and tasks will vary significantly and will not require any late night work.

Fire Steward

The safety of our guests is of paramount importance, and we have established a number of safety procedures to ensure this. Fire stewards will be familiar with these, and will be in charge of enforcing such rules during the ball.

Hit Squad Member

Members of the squad will be appointed to areas where additional staffing is required, or in emergent situations. Staff will need to adapt & respond quickly to new situations.

Clean Up Team Member

Our Clean-Up Team is tasked with maintaining the condition of our college and ensuring the safety of our guests. This will range from collecting glass and litter, to ensuring that our stages remain clean.

Ents Assistant

Due to our busy Ents Schedule, our committee require a small team of assistants to help with our silent disco and other entertainments later on in the event.

Concierge (first half only)

This role is required to help the other workers and staff to enter the ball from our concierge desk. You will need to ensure that all personnel entering the site have the required permissions. This will include communicating with our porters, Staffing and Security department.

Paid worker application: Apply here to work in a paid position for the night of the Ball!

icketing

Tickets for Christ’s May Ball are sold in three different categories, as follows:

Standard - £155

Experience Christ’s Ball 2018!

Queue Jump - £165

Skip the main queue and enter the ball first!

Dining - £185

The full ball experience, with early entry and a 4-course meal!

There will be a subsidised processing fee in addition to the price of the ticket and there will also be an option to donate to one of the charities we are supporting this year.

Christ's Students:

Tickets for Christ's students will be available from Midday on Feb 3rd.
Please read the policy document or ticket event page carefully to understand the ticketing process.
ROUND 1 (12:00 3rd Feb - 08:00 4th Feb): Christ's students can apply for one ticket for themselves and, separately, an additional ticket for a guest.
ROUND 2 (08:00 - 12:00 4th Feb): Christ's students can apply for an additional 2 guest tickets.
Please note that the availability of guest tickets is limited and not guaranteed. Sometime shortly after application, you will be notified if you were successful (or not) and can then proceed to payment.

Christ's Fellows/Staff:

Tickets for Christ's Fellows/Staff will be available from Midday on Feb 4th.
You would have received communication of a shared password and a separate event link from the one above, in which you can go on and apply for tickets. You can apply for up to 4 tickets.

Christ's Alumni:

Christ's Alumni who have been successful in ticket application can go on to claim their tickets and pay from Midday on Feb 3rd.
You will have been sent a separate event link to the one above, in which you can use your unique codes we have sent you. Please select the tickets you have been allocated.

Everyone Else:

You may apply for up to 2 tickets, from Midday on Feb 4th., using the event link above. These tickets are subject to availability.

Please note that only those 18 and over will be permitted to attend the ball.
PAYMENT DEADLINE: 17:00 at 12 Feb 2018.
